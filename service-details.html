Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for a tournament?

Simply click on the tournament you want to join, fill out the registration form, and pay any entry fees. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.

What games are supported?

We support all major esports titles including Valorant, PUBG, FIFA, Fortnite, League of Legends, CS:GO, and many more. If your game isn't listed, contact us to discuss custom tournament options.

How are prizes distributed?

Prizes are distributed automatically within 7 business days after tournament completion. Winners will receive payment via their preferred method (PayPal, bank transfer, etc.).

Can I host my own tournament?

Yes! Our Tournament Hosting service allows you to create and manage your own tournaments. Choose a package that fits your needs and we'll handle all the technical aspects.

What if I need to cancel my registration?

You can cancel your registration up to 24 hours before the tournament starts for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to our refund policy.
